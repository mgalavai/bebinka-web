Four chairs art studio

In 2016 I began conducting workshops and master classes,

sharing my extensive knowledge while living in Malaysia, merging European and Asian art perspectives. Eventually, this led to creating my art studio, where I developed a teaching system that engages both adults and children, fostering their passion for drawing. I named it "4 Chairs" as this optimal group size ensures everyone's comfort and personalized attention. Relocated to Poland, "4 Chairs" thrived for years, offering indoor activities and outdoor drawing sessions in nature and urban settings.
