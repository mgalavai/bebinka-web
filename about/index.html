My name is Lena and I am an artist, based in Göteborg, Sweden.

Holding a master's degree in graphic design and pedagogy, my true passion is art. Over 15 years, I've worked on projects involving classical oil painting, watercolors, fashion illustrations, and game character design, among others.

I've explored diverse art forms while living in different countries, enhancing my skills through local techniques, courses, and workshops. This journey sparked my appreciation for Chinese traditional ink painting, which I learned from a famous Chinese artist in Malaysia. Similarly, my passion for dry pastels developed, studying under European masters in the Netherlands and Italy. Dry pastels remain my main medium and make up most of my work.

In 2016, I began conducting master classes to share my extensive knowledge. This teaching activity eventually evolved into my own art studio, where I've effectively taught both children and adults using my unique approach.

Being a member of various artist foundations and unions, and participating in exhibitions and events, I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations.
